# KANSO — Platform Overview

KANSO is an end-to-end client-acquisition platform purpose-built for franchise consulting. It carries a candidate from the instant they raise their hand on a paid ad all the way to a closed placement — and it removes the friction, the guesswork, and the dropped handoffs at every step in between. Most teams run this funnel on a booking tool *plus* a CRM *plus* a spreadsheet *plus* three reps' memories. KANSO collapses all of that into a single system that always knows where every lead is and what the next move should be.

The platform is organized around five engines, all sharing one source of truth.

---

## 1. The Smart Booking Widget

This is the front door, and it's where KANSO's biggest structural advantage lives: **the lead never re-enters a thing they already told us.**

When someone fills out a Facebook lead form, their name, email, phone, and liquid-capital answer are captured the instant they submit and carried straight through to the booking page. By the time they land on the scheduler, the page already knows who they are — it greets them by name and has their details pre-populated. Every other booking widget on the market makes a warm, ready-to-act lead stop and re-type their information; that re-entry is exactly where motivated people stall and drop. KANSO eliminates that step entirely, which is the single highest-leverage way to lift booking rate at the top of the funnel.

From there the experience is engineered to convert:

- A **guided, Typeform-style flow** that asks one thing at a time instead of dumping a wall of fields.
- A **recommended time** surfaced first — the smartest single slot — so the easy path is one tap, with alternatives and a full calendar one click away for anyone who wants to choose.
- A deliberate **select-then-confirm** step, so a candidate locks in the *right* time on purpose rather than mis-tapping into the wrong one.
- **Brand-aware routing**: each franchise brand has its own booking page, copy, calendar, and rep assignment, so one platform powers many offers.
- **Intelligent rep assignment** behind the scenes — round-robin and investment-level routing — plus real-time Google Calendar availability so a candidate can only book times the assigned rep is actually free.
- **Speed and scarcity** done right: availability is cached so the page loads instantly, and slot display is tuned to create healthy urgency without ever showing a time that isn't real.

The moment a booking lands, KANSO creates the calendar event, sends the Meet link and a branded confirmation email, fires the ad-platform conversion events, and drops the appointment onto the team's dashboard — automatically.

---

## 2. The Prospecting Engine

Leads are worthless if they sit. KANSO's prospecting engine makes sure they never do.

Every lead from every source — Facebook, the booking page, GoHighLevel, Calendly — flows into one unified **Leads** view the instant it arrives, tagged with the brand it came in for, its source, the candidate's liquid-capital band, and its current stage. The team gets notified the moment a new lead or booking hits, so nobody finds out a day late.

What makes it a *prospecting* engine and not just a list is that KANSO **scores intent based on behavior**. It watches the signals that actually predict a close — did they confirm their appointment by text, did they show up, did they open the questionnaire, did they revisit the page — and surfaces the warm, engaged, ready-to-move candidates to the top. Reps spend their time on the people leaning in, not guessing.

From the same view, the team can **filter by brand or by rep**, slice the pipeline however they need, and **bulk-tag a selection** to hand straight off to a GoHighLevel workflow — so a new outreach sequence is two clicks, not a manual export. Candidates who need a meeting rebooked get routed to a dedicated **"Needs Reschedule"** bucket so a fallen appointment becomes a clear next action instead of a lost lead.

---

## 3. The Nurture Engine

Not every candidate moves in a straight line, and the money is in the follow-up. The nurture engine keeps KANSO in consistent, organized contact with candidates who are still deciding — the long-tail relationships that competitors let go cold.

This is how a deal that wasn't ready in week one still gets across the finish line in week six: the platform keeps those candidates visible, scheduled, and worked, so "I'll think about it" turns into a placement instead of silence.

---

## 4. CQ Recovery

The CQ is the step that moves a candidate forward — and an uncompleted CQ is a stalled deal. KANSO's **CQ Recovery** engine exists to make sure not a single one slips through.

It maintains a live, **scored recovery queue** of everyone who received a CQ but hasn't returned it, ranked by a commitment-stack score that weighs every engagement signal the platform has on that person — confirmations, show, page visits, email opens. Reps work the highest-probability recoveries first, can **resend** in a click, and can **snooze** the ones that need to wait — so the queue is always a clean, prioritized worklist rather than a pile of forgotten names. The result is simple: every outstanding CQ gets chased, intelligently, until it comes back.

---

## 5. Analytics for Everything

KANSO measures the whole funnel, not just vanity totals. The analytics layer rolls up an **executive summary** and the numbers that actually run the business — booked, showed, no-show, **show rate**, CQ sent and returned, and pipeline/revenue value — plus the operational detail underneath it: performance by consultant, the funnel from lead to placement, and even the best time slots for getting CQs back. Because every source feeds one system, the reporting is finally trustworthy — one set of numbers instead of three tools disagreeing with each other.

---

## The Connective Tissue

What ties it together is just as important as the five engines:

- **One unified record.** Native bookings, Calendly, and GoHighLevel appointments are merged into a single meetings view, so a rep's dashboard reflects reality no matter where a meeting originated.
- **Deep integrations.** Two-way with GoHighLevel (contacts, conversations, opportunities, tags, workflows), Facebook lead ads, Calendly, Google Calendar, and email — KANSO sits in the middle and keeps everyone in sync.
- **AI where it counts.** Inbound replies are read by an AI classifier that determines whether a candidate actually confirmed, declined, or went quiet — so the dashboard reflects true intent instead of tripping over quoted email boilerplate.
- **Real workflow tools.** Reschedule a no-show, hand an appointment to another rep without notifying the client, view and reply to the live HighLevel SMS thread from inside the contact card, mark a disposition and change it later — the day-to-day actions a real sales team needs, built in.
- **Multi-brand, multi-user, controlled.** Per-brand booking pages and routing, team roles and granular permissions, and an admin layer that lets the operator configure availability, branding, notifications, and access without touching code.

---

## What Makes It Different

Most tools in this space optimize one slice — a prettier calendar, a louder CRM, a fancier dashboard. KANSO's edge is that it owns the **entire path** and removes friction at every handoff: zero re-entry at booking, behavior-based prioritization in prospecting, disciplined follow-up in nurture, relentless recovery on the CQ, and honest numbers across all of it. A lead enters at the top and the platform always knows exactly where they are and what to do next — so motivated candidates convert, and the ones who need time don't fall through the cracks.

That's the whole thesis: **stop losing deals to friction and forgetfulness, and turn the funnel into a system.**
